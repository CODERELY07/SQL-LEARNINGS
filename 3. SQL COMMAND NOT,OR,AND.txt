
show customers where birth_date is greater than 1990-01-01 or points > 1000 and the state is VA
- SELECT * FROM customers WHERE birth_date > '1990-01-01' OR * (points > 1000 AND state = 'VA')

note: The AND operator has higher precedence than OR so the and operator will execute first but the parenthesis
can overwrite it, this means parenthesis is the highest precedence.

show customers where birth date is not greater than 1990-01-01 OR points greater than 1000
- SELECT * FROM customers WHERE NOT (birth_date > '1990-01-01 OR points > 1000
note: Using NOT keyword affect all such as greater than is now less than or equal , less than
is now greater than or equal and the OR will be AND

We can put arithmetic in the after WHERE clause
Table: order_items

show the items for order #6 where the total price is greater than 30
- SELECT * FROM order_items WHERE order_id = 6 AND unit_price * quantity > 30
